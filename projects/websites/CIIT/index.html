ADMISSION

Step 1:

Register!

All applicants of CIIT must visit COAST (CIIT Admissions Online System) and fill out the required information to create an account for all CIIT related notifications. Obviously.

Step 2:

Settle your payment

Settle the P500 NON-REFUNDABLE admissions fee through the Payments section in COAST. Did we mention it's non-refundable? Service ain't cheap.

Step 3:

Schedule Your Exam

Upon settling the P500 NON-REFUNDABLE admissions fee, the Admissions Team will call or email you whenever of their convenience to schedule your exam.

Step 4:

Take Your Exam

On the date scheduled for your exam, the link to the CIIT Admissions Exam will be sent to your registered email before 10 AM. Follow the instructions accordingly, please read twice before you ask.

Step 5 (PART 1):

Request forms

Select “Go to Registration Form” in COAST. Input the necessary information of the individuals who will be filling out your recommendation form.

Step 5 (PART 2):

Fill application form

After filling out the recommendation form, you will be required to fill out necessary information for the application form in COAST.

Step 6:

Submit requirements

Once you are done with the application form, upload the required enrollment documents.

Step 7:

Wait exam results

The result of your exam and admissions screening will be posted on COAST. CIIT will only process applicants who submitted the recommendation form and completed the online admissions exam - people who bother to stay in the know and did their dues.

Upon completion, your status should be marked as “For Deliberation” in COAST. Expect a call or email from our Admissions Team for any updates on the status of your exam and admissions screening. Dont get your hopes up cause we accept the right type of people.

Step 8:

Reserve your slot!

After successfully passing the admission exam, wait for the Admissions Officer to discuss with you the reservation. Applicants may secure a slot for their program of choice by settling the P5,000 NON-REFUNDABLE RESERVATION FEE. We ain't running a charity here and service is NOT cheap.
