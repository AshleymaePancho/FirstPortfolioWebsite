About Me

During my time at CIIT College, we had programming and web design classes, which I genuinely fell in love with. I decided to follow this newfound interest and learn new things for a change. Out of all my possible options, professions involving programming is something I find the most appealing.

I'm aiming to be a software or web developer after ZUITT Bootcamp. I also plan on developing games and making animations. Video games are an integral part of my life - ever since I was a kid, I would play point-and-click pc games with my mom, handheld and mobile games with my dad, and from console games to online browser games with my siblings. Animation is also important to me as I used to watch a lot of cartoons as a kid which gave me an escape from reality.

I will push myself to learn what I can here at ZUITT because I really feel a greater accomplishment here than in animation or art. I want to have a stable job where I can give back to my parents and feel no financial pressure with pursuing other paths like game development and animation in my own time. And that stable job might as well be something I find enjoyment and fulfillment in.
